Introduction:
SuperHeroes Hub respects intellectual property rights. This policy outlines our procedures for addressing copyright infringement claims.

Submitting a Takedown Request:

To submit a notice of infringement, please contact nmurad4@uwo.ca.
Include the following in your notice: What you would like to take down and your reasoning.
Procedure:

We will promptly investigate and respond to valid takedown requests.
A record of all takedown requests will be maintained.
Dispute Claims:

Users may dispute claims by reasonable means.
Dispute claims will be reviewed, and a record will be maintained.
Contact:
For any inquiries related to this policy, contact us at nmurad4@uwo.ca.
