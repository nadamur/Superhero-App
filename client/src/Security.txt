Security and Privacy Policy. Introduction:
Welcome to SuperHeroes Hub! This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect user data.

Data Collection:

We collect usernames, emails, passwords when users sign up. We collect reviews with the review date and the user who wrote it when a review is written. We collect the date a user modifies or creates a list.
Data Usage:

The collected data is used for displaying lists in an orderly fashion and keeping track of reviews.
We do not share user data with third parties.
Security Measures:

Authentication and authorization measures in order to prevent outside sources from accessing our data.

Updates:
This policy is subject to change, and any updates will be reflected on this page
