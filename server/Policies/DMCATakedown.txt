DMCA Takedown Procedure

Workflow Document:

Submission: Users submit notices to nmurad4@uwo.ca.
Review: The received notice is reviewed for validity.
Takedown: If valid, content is temporarily removed pending resolution.
Logging Tools:

Takedown Request Log:
Date | Requester | Alleged Infringement | Status
Infringement Notice Log:
Date | Sender | Alleged Infringement | Status
Dispute Claim Log:
Date | Claimant | Disputed Content | Status
Disabling and Restoring Tools:

Disable Content Tool: Temporarily hides content with alleged copyright violations. Temporarily disables user accounts.
Restore Content Tool: Restores content after resolution.
